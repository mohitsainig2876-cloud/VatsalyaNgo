About Our Mission

Vatsalya NGo is a non-profit organization focused on sustainable development projects in rural and urban slum areas. We collaborate with local communities to create long-term impact through education, health camps, and livelihood programs.

Vision

Build a world where every child has access to education, nutrition, and a safe home.

Mission

Empower vulnerable families with resources, training, and support to break the cycle of poverty.

Values

Transparency, inclusion, dignity, and community-led change are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Key Causes

Child Education

After-school learning centers, scholarships, and digital literacy programs.

Healthcare Camps

Regular health check-ups, vaccinations, and maternal health awareness drives.

Emergency Relief

Food, shelter, and essentials for families affected by natural disasters.
